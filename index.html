The Three Levels of Craft

A self-review guide for engineers and designers. Ask these questions every time you ship.

How to use this. Before you mark a feature "done," walk through the three levels. Level 1 is table stakes. Level 2 is where good products separate from average ones. Level 3 is where you build systems that make the product feel effortless. You won't hit Level 3 on every ticket, but you should always know which level you stopped at, and why.

Scope note. This is not a style guide, the design system owns how the product looks. These levels are about how it works, and looks only enter where they change what a person understands or trusts.
